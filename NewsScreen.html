ERC take part in viral challenge

You may have seen other groups taking part in a challenge, of virtually passing a toilet roll in various ways. In a bid to keep everyone at home, but still feel connected we decided to do it - but with a twist.You can watch our variation of it through either the YouTube video or on the fixtures page.

All races cancelled/postponed until further notice

As with other sproting events happening around the world, all running races have been either cancelled for this year or postponed indefinitely. More information regarding can be found on the piece put out by Athletics Northern Ireland We hope our Spooktacular will will go ahead this year especially as this our 10th anniversary year.

Awards night postponed

Our annual awards night has been postponed due to the ongoing coronavirus pandemic. Once the condition eases our board committee will arrange a new date with The Westville Hotel.It is looking like the awards will not be till around December time.
